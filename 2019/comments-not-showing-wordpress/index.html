Skip to the end if you’ve already checked that your comments are turned on in your theme settings, Wordpress settings, and on the individual post that you are trying to see comments on.

  1. Make sure in your Genesis Theme Settings your comments are enabled.


  1. Make sure your Wordpress comments are turned on in your Discussion Settings. Your settings should look similar to this:


  1. Make sure you have turned on your comments in the individual post

If Nothing Else Works

I know of 2 possible reasons:

  1. If you’ve got this checkbox checked in your Discussion Settings:

This could create a situation where some of your posts are showing comments while others do not. Took me 2 hours to realize that this was my problem!

  1. Your theme may not have comments written into the page/post templates. This means that the comments_template() (link)) hook is not present, or in the case of Genesis, the genesis_do_comments() hook is not present (if you are using Genesis, you will not have this issue unless you have manually edited the Genesis files, which you should not be doing!).
